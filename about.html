My Journey

My fascination with game development began when I was 12, deconstructing games to understand how they worked. This curiosity eventually led me to pursue a Computer Science degree with a focus on AI and game programming.

Throughout my career, I've specialized in developing complex AI systems for games, from strategic decision-making for NPCs to procedural content generation. I'm particularly passionate about creating gameplay systems that adapt to player behavior and provide unique experiences.

My approach to game programming combines technical precision with creative problem-solving. I believe that great games emerge from the seamless integration of compelling mechanics, responsive controls, and intelligent systems that surprise and delight players.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy participating in game jams, contributing to open source projects, and exploring emerging technologies in the game development space.
